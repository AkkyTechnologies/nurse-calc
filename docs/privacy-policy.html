Titr8

Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 12, 2026

Titr8 is built to work entirely offline. This page describes, in full, how the app handles data — because the honest answer is that it doesn't.

What we collect

Nothing. Titr8 does not collect, store, transmit, or share any data about you or your patients. The app has no analytics, no crash reporting, no advertising SDKs, and makes no network requests of any kind. Every calculation — dosage, IV drip rate, flow rate, and pediatric dosing — runs locally on your device.

What we store

Only on your device. Your saved Favorites (medication concentrations and pediatric dosing guidelines you choose to save) are stored locally using standard iOS app storage. This data never leaves your device, is never sent to Akky Technologies or anyone else, and is deleted if you delete the app.

Third parties

None. Titr8 does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or data-processing service.

Children's privacy

Not applicable. Because the app collects no data from any user, it does not collect data from children either.

Changes to this policy

If this ever changes, we'll update this page and note it in the app's version history.
